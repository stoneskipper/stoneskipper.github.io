Reclaimed Ladder Coat Rack

This coat rack was made from an old wooden ladder I pulled from the Ohio River after a flood. It had clearly traveled — water-worn, dented, still holding onto traces of green paint and rusted hardware. I carefully dried it, cleaned it up without removing the story etched into its grain, and added a few handmade hooks from salvaged hardware.

It's not trying to hide what it is — a survivor of hard labor, repurposed with care. This is one of my favorite things I’ve made, not because it was easy, but because it reminds me that beautiful things don’t need to be perfect. Just honest.
